--- author: Marielle Machacek, Lucia Cherep, Carly Scott, Anabelle Laurent, Kimberly Ferguson title: Exploration of National Electronic Injury Surveillance System Database topic: "Presentations" layout: post root: ../../../ --- ## Exploration of National Electronic Injury Surveillance System Database The National Electronic Injury Surveillance System has been an essential tool in identifying the number and severity of consumer product-related injuries to persons treated in hospital emergency departments in the United States. We will be exploring the database to examine potential relationships between patient characteristics, product characteristics, and injury characteristics.
